## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's your photography style?**  
My work focuses on natural light, cinematic storytelling, and capturing authentic moments with a documentary-style approach.

**Do you travel for shoots?**  
Yes! I'm available for destination photography and love exploring new locations.

**What's included in a session?**  
Each package is customized, but typically includes pre-shoot consultation, the photo session, professional editing, and a digital gallery of high-resolution images.

**How do I book?**  
Send me an email with your project details, preferred dates, and any specific requirements. I'll respond with availability and pricing information.

**What's your turnaround time?**  
Edited photos are typically delivered within 2-3 weeks, depending on the project scope.
